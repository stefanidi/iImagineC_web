Flow is a simple, yet elegant, app that instantly displays your daily activities for the past 7 days.

No Battery Drain

Flow does not drain battery and does not need to run in the background to actively track your activities. In fact, Flow will show your activity summary for the past 7 days as soon as you start using it.

Automatic Sleep Detect

Flow will automatically detect your sleep duration and times, with no need for user input. Simply place your phone on the bedside table when you go to sleep and pick it up when you wake in the morning.

Walk, Run, Steps

Flow will track your Walking and Running time. Display the number of steps you take throughout the day.

Activity Level

Flow will estimate your general activity time through the day.

Drive or Cycle

Flow detects how long you drive or cycle.

Works Instantly

Flow uses latest hardware in the iPhone 5s and will instantly display your activities for the past seven days from day one.
